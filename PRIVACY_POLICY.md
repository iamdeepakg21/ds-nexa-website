# Privacy Policy - DS Nexa Solutions

**Last Updated: March 2026**

At **DS Nexa Solutions**, we believe in the absolute transparency and protection of your data. We build software for the future, and that future is privacy-first.

## 1. Transparency First
We value your privacy as much as our own code. We don't sell your data, we don't rent your attention, and we don't track you across the web.

## 2. Data We Collect
We only collect what you explicitly give us. When you fill out our contact form, we receive your:
- Name
- Email Address
- Project Details

This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and potentially work together on your project.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We use a small number of trusted partners for operational purposes:
- **Web3Forms**: For contact form processing.
- **Vercel**: For high-performance global hosting.

These partners are GDPR and CCPA compliant and do not use your information for their own purposes.

## 4. Cookies & Tracking
Our website uses minimal, essential cookies only. We don't use invasive tracking pixels or fingerprinting. Any analytics we use are anonymous and do not link to your identity.

## 5. Your Rights
You are the owner of your data. You have the right to:
- Access the data we have about you.
- Request that we delete your data.
- Withdraw your consent at any time.

To exercise these rights, please email us at [contact@dsnexasolutions.co.in](mailto:contact@dsnexasolutions.co.in).

## 6. Security
We treat your data with the same engineering rigor we apply to our high-scale distributed systems. All communications are encrypted (SSL/TLS), and our infrastructure is monitored for security threats 24/7.
